I am a Ph.D. candidate at University of Toronto where I work with Dr. Dimitrios Hatzinakos. My research interests focus on machine learning, signal processing and NLP areas. During Ph.D., I am mainly working on biometric area to suggest the unique and time-stable verification system with the physiological signals.

In the past, I have great chances to work with an amazing set of researchers and companies. I achieved a Master's degree at Texas A&M University where I was advised by Dr. Javier Jo. Before starting a Ph.D., I worked at Hyundai Mobis as a Research Engineer. During pursuing a Ph.D. degree, I also interned at Amazon Science as an Applied Scientist Intern. After Ph.D. graduation, I will join the Toronto-based Amazon Science as an Applied Scientist.

For more details, check my resume or hit me up on my email.
