I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon Science in Toronto, working on information retrieval, generative AI, LLM, RAG and representation learning. My research interests focus on machine learning, signal processing, NLP and computer vision areas.

In the past, I have great chances to work with an amazing set of researchers and companies. I achieved a Master's degree at Texas A&M University where I was advised by Prof. Javier Jo. Before starting a Ph.D., I worked at Hyundai Mobis as a Research Engineer. During pursuing a Ph.D. degree at University of Toronto, I was supervised by Prof. Dimitrios Hatzinakos and worked on biometric area and human activity recognition with machine learning techniques.

For more details, check my resume or hit me up on my email.
